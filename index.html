Hi! I'm Kayley an interdisciplinary artist based in Harlem, NY!

Kayley Chery is a multidisciplinary visual artist and equity-minded storyteller whose practice weaves together education, technology, and social justice.

She explores how creative interventions can foster connection and amplify marginalized voices through the intersection of art & interactive media.

As an NYU Interactive Media Arts student, Kayley invites viewers into spaces of collective imagination, asking us how art can serve as both a mirror and catalyst for social transformation!
